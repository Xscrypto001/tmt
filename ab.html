Who We Are

Timatania Enterprises Ltd is a freight forwarding company,
established in 2019 in the Coastal, Port based city of Mombasa.
We boast of a team of qualified professionals, who coupled
with the required training and certifications,have worked in
logistics related fields for well over 10 years and gathered up
adequate experiences to handle your cargo with the speed &
delicacy it requires.

Our Vision

Create a network within East & Central Africa that reaches global heights

Our Mission

Mission:- To be a one-stop logistics provider who thrives coherently on
our core values;Efficiency, Transparency, Integrity & Commitment. Excellence
through customer satisfaction

Core Values

1. Efficiency: Time & Money is always of the essence and we
strive to achieve timely feedback to queries, seamless delivery of
consignments and competitive rates that will see our customers achieve
market dominance.
2. Transparency: Avoid unnecessary liability by creating a safe environment
of transparency both with our employees and our customers.
3. Integrity: Creating a culture of integrity and accountability not only
improves efficiency, it also generates a respectful, enjoyable and life-giving
setting in which to work.
4. Commitment: Experienced staff committed solely to meeting customer satisfaction.
